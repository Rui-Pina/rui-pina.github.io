Fisher delivers pragmatic wisdom on risk management and the psychology of conservative investing. This book speaks directly to investors who understand that the best investment returns come not from heroic bets but from disciplined, thoughtful decision-making paired with careful risk assessment.

The title captures the essence perfectly: a well-constructed conservative portfolio allows you to sleep peacefully, knowing you won't be blindsided by catastrophic losses. Fisher's emphasis on understanding what you're investing in and maintaining emotional discipline resonates with timeless investing principles.

The book offers valuable perspective on portfolio construction and the temperament required for long-term success. While not groundbreaking, it provides solid counsel that has aged well. Recommended for investors seeking peace of mind alongside reasonable returns.
